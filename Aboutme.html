about me

Hey, welcome to my website!

I'm António and I hope you are feeling great today! :)

I'm an Artist, Music Producer and Sound Engineer based in Sintra, Portugal.

I've always been fascinated with music and it's infinite potential for connection and expression.

In my early teens I fell into the rabbit hole of songwriting, music production, sound manipulation and mixing.

My passion quickly became an obsession, as I spent most of my time experimenting with new techniques, gear and Ideas.

Nowadays, I get to help clients achieve the sound they imagine in their heads.

I'm all about dynamics, contrast, warm textures, punchy transients. I strive for fat mixes with plenty of musical moments to make the song/production shine.

I like all kinds of music, but some genres I really enjoy working with are: Indie/Alternative/Dream/Psych Pop and Rock, Hip-hop, Electronic/dance music, World Music (...)

Over the years I've had the pleasure and luck of working with extremely talented and inspiring people.

I'm now looking to grow that list and explore different soundscapes!
