Metamito

Songwritting / Recording /Producing / Mixing
Psych-pop / Alternative


Metamito is my main solo music project / alter ego.
Here I do all the song-writting, recording and music production, aswell as mixing on some tracks.
I perform live as a "one-man-show", using a loopstation and various instruments, having toured Portugal, including Festivals suchs as "Super Bock Super Rock" and "Festival Iminente", aswell as some venues in Spain.

SOUND DESIGN / POST-PRODUCTION / OST

SGC#1 - Videogame for the Nintendo Switch

In 2021 I worked with "Nerd Monkeys", an Indie Videogame Studio from Portugal. For this project I did sound design for the Hub Menu of "SGC - Short Game Collection #1", a videogame for the Nintendo Switch, aswell as the background music and ambience.
During my collaboration with the company, I also created sound designs for a secret mini-game.

Also in the context of SGC, I produced a track that incorporated the company's jingle, which ended up being used for the game's promotion and advertisement.

Fig.gif Short Loops - Sound design / Composition

Me and my talented animator friend @Fig.gif collaborated on a series of short loops. He created the visuals and afterwards I did sound, be it sound design, foley or producing a short music loop to accompany the images.

Breach - Shortfilm

"Breach" is a shortfilm directed by Rita Monteiro.For this project I did some some design / fx and composed an original soundtrack.It's a low budget sci-fi about a dystopian world with teleportation devices.Went with a mysterious / uncanny vibe for the OST, while keeping it playful and not too serious.
